tiles_along_dir¶

Groups of tiles to be stitched along a given direction.

You need to send to this generator a tuple containing:
  • a list for sorting the pandas.DataFrame, such as ['Z', 'Y', 'X']
  • an axis for grouping, such as 'Y'
